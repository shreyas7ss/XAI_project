# AI Usage Disclosure

This document records all uses of AI-assisted tools in the development of this
replication project, in compliance with course and academic-integrity policies.

---

## Tool Used

**Tool:** Antigravity (Google DeepMind AI assistant)
**Model:** Gemini-based agentic coding assistant
**Date of use:** March 2026

## Prompts Used

The following prompt was provided in full to generate the initial codebase:

> "You are an expert ML engineer helping replicate a NeurIPS 2022 paper.
> **Paper:** OpenXAI: Towards a Transparent Evaluation of Model Explanations
> **Goal:** Generate a complete, runnable Python project that loads pretrained
> ANN models and datasets from the openxai library, runs all 7 explainers on
> 2 datasets, computes 5 benchmark metrics, and saves result tables as CSVs
> and figures as PNGs …"
>
> *(Full prompt specified exact file names, function signatures, metric names,
> explainer parameter dicts, visualization types, CLI arguments, and
> documentation requirements.)*

## What Was Modified by the Team

- Actual metric numbers in `report/phase2_summary.md` (placeholders filled
  after running the experiment).
- Team member names and contributions in `README.md`.
- Any dataset-specific hyperparameter tuning discovered during validation runs.
- Observations and conclusions in the Phase 2 summary report.

---

## What Was NOT AI-Generated

- The intellectual analysis and interpretation of results.
- The literature review and comparison with paper values.
- The conclusion paragraph drawing upon domain knowledge.
- The experimental validation judgement (deciding whether results match the
  paper's reported values).
